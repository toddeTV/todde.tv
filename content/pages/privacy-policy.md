## 2. Overview of Data Processing (_Übersicht der Datenverarbeitung_)

This website is a static portfolio site that does not collect, store, or process personal data on its own servers. There are no user accounts, no contact forms, no first-party analytics or tracking, and no advertising. The hosting provider (Cloudflare) may collect aggregated, anonymized analytics data as described in section 3.

Data processing related to website visits occurs through the hosting infrastructure described below. If you contact us by email, additional processing applies as described in section 6.

## 4. Cookies

This website does not set any cookies on its own. Cloudflare may set **technically necessary cookies** (e.g. `__cf_bm` for bot detection) to protect the website from malicious traffic. These cookies are required for the legitimate operation of the website and do not require consent pursuant to § 25 (2) TDDDG (_Telekommunikation-Digitale-Dienste-Datenschutz-Gesetz_).

No analytics cookies, marketing cookies, or tracking cookies are used.

## 8. Your Rights (_Ihre Rechte_)

Under the GDPR (_DSGVO_), you have the following rights regarding the processing of your personal data:

- **Right of access** (_Auskunftsrecht_) - Art. 15 GDPR: You have the right to request information about your stored personal data.
- **Right to rectification** (_Recht auf Berichtigung_) - Art. 16 GDPR: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
- **Right to erasure** (_Recht auf Löschung_) - Art. 17 GDPR: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
- **Right to restriction of processing** (_Recht auf Einschränkung der Verarbeitung_) - Art. 18 GDPR: You have the right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data.
- **Right to data portability** (_Recht auf Datenportabilität_) - Art. 20 GDPR: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
- **Right to object** (_Widerspruchsrecht_) - Art. 21 GDPR: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data based on Art. 6 (1) (e) or (f) GDPR at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address listed above.
